## üíª PC Playing Instructions
*Play like a pro using your keyboard and mouse.*

*   **Move Left:** Press the **[ A ]** key.
*   **Move Right:** Press the **[ D ]** key.
*   **Jump:** Press the **[ W ]** key.
*   **Attack (Weapon):** Use **[ Left Click ]** on your mouse.
*   **Kick:** Use **[ Right Click ]** on your mouse.
*   **Throw Weapon:** Press the **[ Spacebar ]** (Only works for *Throwable* weapons like the Axe, Dagger, Spear, etc.).

## üì± Mobile Playing Instructions
*Optimized for landscape mode. Rotate your phone to start.*

*   **Movement:** Use the **Floating Joystick** on the **left side** of the screen.
    *   Slide left/right to walk.
    *   Slide the joystick **Up** to jump.
*   **Attack (Weapon):** Tap the **[ Punch Icon ]** button on the right.
*   **Kick:** Tap the **[ Leg Icon ]** button on the right.
*   **Throw Weapon:** Tap the **[ Orange Throw Button ]**. 
    *   *Note: This button only appears if you have selected a weapon labeled as "Throwable".*

## ‚öîÔ∏è Special Weapon Tips
*   **Heavies (Mace/Axe):** Very high damage but slow swings. Time your hits carefully!
*   **Speedsters (Nunchaku/Knuckles):** Very fast attacks. Stay close to the enemy and keep hitting them.
*   **Polearms (Spear):** Use the long reach to hit enemies before they can get close to you.
*   **Throwables:** If an enemy is running away, press **Space** or the **Throw button**. The weapon will fly, hit them, and magnetically return to your hand!
